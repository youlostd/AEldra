[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]1. General Information

The event is for Lv30+ players only.
One hit registers 1 damage to the boss' HP and you receive points which you will be able to see at the 
bottom left corner UI, above your HP bar.

You can redeem the points that you collected at the Uriel for several chests.
Whilst attacking this boss, your attack speed is reduced by 100.


[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=210; CENTER_ALIGN]

[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]2. Event Instructions:

Shadow Warrior will appear in the neautral areas of Orc Valley, Mount Sohan, 
Doyyumhwaji Paper and Yongbi Desert (2 for each channel).
Around the Shadow Warrior, the PvP mode is disabled.
For every hit on a Shadow Warrior you get 1 Point. 

Brutal Shadow Warrior will appear in the beta areas of Cape Dragon Fire, Thunder Mountains, 
Nephrite Bay and Guatama Cliff (2 for each channel).
Around the Brutal Shadow Warrior, the PvP mode is enabled.
For every hit on a Brutal Shadow Warrior you get 2 Points.


[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=400; CENTER_ALIGN]

[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]3. Hit Counter Rewards

You can then exchange your points at Uriel for either a Bronze Decimus Chets, Silver Decimus Chest, 
Golden Decimus Chest or a Magnificent Decimus Chest.

Warning: The points will disappear as soon as the Event closes, so make sure to redeem them on time.

*500 Points: Bronze Decimus Chest
*1000 Points: Silver Decimus Chest
*2000 Points: Golden Decimus Chest
*5000 Points: Magnificent Decimus Chest
