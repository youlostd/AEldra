FISHING JIGSAW[ENTER]
Go fishing for Fish Jigsaw Chests! Each chest contains a random jigsaw[ENTER]
piece. Complete the jigsaw in the fewest attempts possible and earn[ENTER]
yourself an amazing event prize.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
Left-click on a chest to open it. Drag the jigsaw piece to the desired[ENTER]
location with your cursor and place it there. Right-click to discard a[ENTER]
piece if you don't like the look of it or it doesn't fit.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
The fewer attempts needed, the better the reward you'll receive:[ENTER]
10 or fewer: Golden Fish Chest (L)[ENTER]
11 to 24: Golden Fish Chest (M)[ENTER]
25 or more: Golden Fish Chest (S)[ENTER]
[ENTER]
DELUXE[ENTER]
If you have a Fishing Book, you'll have a chance of fishing out a Fish[ENTER]
Jigsaw Chest Deluxe. These chests contain special deluxe jigsaw pieces.[ENTER]
Just four of these are enough to claim victory and the Golden Fish Chest[ENTER]
(L) is as good as yours.
