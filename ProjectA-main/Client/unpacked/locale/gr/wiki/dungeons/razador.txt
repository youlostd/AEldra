[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18]The Phases:
[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=140; CENTER_ALIGN]
[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Level 1

[X_PADDING=20]Kill all 8 of the Ignators. Each Ignator drops a Cog Wheel.
[X_PADDING=20]Drag & Drop the Cog Wheel to a Seal to destroy it. All 8 Seals must be destroyed.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Level 2

[X_PADDING=20]Kill all Monsters, then activate the Maat Stone.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Level 3

[X_PADDING=20]Destroy the Metin Stones in the correct order. An arrow indicates the metin that needs to be destroyed.
[X_PADDING=20]When you kill the wrong Metin, the level starts from the beginning.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Level 4 

[X_PADDING=20]Destroy the Metin Stone. At 50% HP, a monster wave will appear. You must first kill all the monsters
[X_PADDING=20]for the metin stone to receive damage again. After the Metin is destroyed, 
[X_PADDING=20]another monster wave appears, which must be killed.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Level 5

[X_PADDING=20]Monster waves are spawned every 20 seconds. If more than 300 monsters are alive, 
[X_PADDING=20]then the whole level restarts.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Level 6

[X_PADDING=20]Kill Razador.
