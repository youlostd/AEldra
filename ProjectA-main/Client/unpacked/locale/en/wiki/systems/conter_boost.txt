[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]1. General Information.
[X_PADDING=20]*The Ward & Boost skills are a unique way to evolve further more your character's defenses and strengths.
[X_PADDING=20]*You can skill up all 8 Ward Skills and 1 Boost Skill to P1.
[X_PADDING=20]*To train a Special Skill from 0 to M1, you'll need to invest 20 Skill Points or read 20 books of the specific skill.
[X_PADDING=20]*To train a Special Skill from M1 to G1, you'll need a total of 55 Ward or Boosts of this specific skill.
[X_PADDING=20]*To train a Special Skill from G1 to P1, you'll need Soul Stone+.
[X_PADDING=20]*Instead of Concentrated Reading & Exorcism Scroll, you now need to use 
[X_PADDING=20]Legendary Concentrated Reading & Legendary Exorcism Scroll.

[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_small.tga; Y=230; CENTER_ALIGN]


[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]1.1 Ward Skills
[X_PADDING=20]The Ward skills reduce the incoming damage from certain abilities which are: 
[X_PADDING=20]*Sword Spin
[X_PADDING=20]*Summon Lightning
[X_PADDING=20]*Dark Strike
[X_PADDING=20]*Fire Arrow
[X_PADDING=20]*Spirit Strike
[X_PADDING=20]*Shooting Dragon
[X_PADDING=20]*Finger Strike
[X_PADDING=20]*Ambush
[X_PADDING=20]The more the ward skill is trained, the less damage you'll receive from it.


[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_small.tga; Y=400; CENTER_ALIGN]

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]1.2 Boost Skills
[X_PADDING=20]The Boost skills increase the outgoing damage from certain abilities which are the following:
[X_PADDING=20]*Warrior Body: Sword Spin.
[X_PADDING=20]*Warrior Mental: Spirit Strike.
[X_PADDING=20]*Sura Weaponry: Finger Strike.
[X_PADDING=20]*Sura Black Magic: Dark Strike.
[X_PADDING=20]*Ninja Blade-Fight: Ambush.
[X_PADDING=20]*Ninja Archery: Fire Arrow.
[X_PADDING=20]*Shaman Healing-Force: Summon Lightning.
[X_PADDING=20]*Shaman Dragon-Force: Shooting Dragon.
[X_PADDING=20]You have only 1 active Boost Skill, depending on your class, as described previously.
[X_PADDING=20]The more the boost skill is trained, the more damage the enemy will receive from it.
