[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]1. General Information

Each player from level 30 can take part in the event.
You need a Fishing Rod, Worm or Paste.
At the top right of the event button you will find the fish puzzle game where you can use the puzzle.
During the event you get 5 background with every successful catch fish puzzle boxes.
With an equipped background Fishing Book one has the chance to have a background Fish Puzzlebox Deluxe. 
(You can buy the Fishing Book from the Itemshop for 3h.) The standard fish puzzle chests contain a random 
puzzle piece. The Deluxebox contains a larger puzzle piece.


[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=250; CENTER_ALIGN]

[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]2. Game Instructions

The goal of the game is to complete the free fields with the pieces of the puzzle with as few moves as possible.
A left click opens the chests.
In the bottom field come the fish puzzle boxes and into the top the Fish Puzzlebox Deluxe.
Left-click on a chest to open it. Drag the piece to the desired location with your cursor and place it there. 
Right-click to discard a piece if you don't like the look of it or it doesn’t fit.


[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=380; CENTER_ALIGN]

[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]3. Round Rewards

When you have finished the puzzle, you get 30,000 exp.
Depending on the necessary attempts you will get one of three golden fish chests.
The fewer attempts needed, the better the reward you’ll receive.

*10 or less: 3x Golden Fish Chest (L).
*11 to 24: 2x Golden Fish Chest (L).
*25 or more: 1x Golden Fish Chest (L).
