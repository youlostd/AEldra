[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]1. The different types of Sashes and how to obtain one

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]1.1 The Shoulder Sashes:
There are 4 different Shoulder Sashes:
*Lord Sash
*Master Sash
*Sovereign Sash
*Royal Sash

The difference between the Shoulder Sashes are only the look.
[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_small.tga; Y=250; CENTER_ALIGN]


[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]1.2 From where the Shoulder Sash?
The Shoulder Sash can be crafted at Theowahdan with different cloath's
The Cloath's can be dropped from the metinstones in the Cape Dragon Fire, Nephrite Bay, 
Thunder Mountains and Gautama Cliff.
There are 4 different cloaths, every cloath is for one Shoulder Sash:
*10x Green Cloath -> Lord Sash
*10x Purple Cloath -> Master Sash
*10x Blue Cloath -> Sovereign Sash
*10x Yellow Cloath -> Royal Sash
To craft the Shoulder Sashes you need 250.000 Yang and 10x cloath of one color, it can't fail.

[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]2. How to improve a Shoulder Sash

[COLOR=ef4f00]The Shoulder Sash has 4 different levels:
*basic
*fine
*noble
*custom

To Shoulder Sashes of the same level can be combinded at Theowahdan to get a higher level.
At the combination process the Shoulder Sash at the top will be upgraded and the one at the bottom will be destroyed.
The upgrade from the Shoulder Sash can fail.
It's possible to add the Shoulder Sashes in the combinationwindow with Drag and Drop or "ALT + left mouseklick".
A custom Shoulder Sash can be combined with a other custom Shoulder Sash to get the maximum 25%.
Each upgrade on the level custom with a other custom Shoulder Sash is 100% and will give you 1% more.

[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]3. How to add a Weapon to the Shoulder Sash

You can add the bonuses of your weapon to your Shoulder Sash.
You can add your weapon to a Shoulder Sash at Theowahdan under Inclusion of Bonuses.
> The Weapon will be destroyed in this process!

The Shoulder Sash will only gives you a specific % of the bonuses.
*Shoulder Sash (basic) will absorb 1% of the bonuses from the weapon.
*Shoulder Sash (fine) will absorb 5% of the bonuses from the weapon.
*Shoulder Sash (noble) will absorb 10% of the bonuses from the weapon.
*Shoulder Sash (custom) will absorb 11 - 25% of the bonuses from the weapon.

The Bonuses will be rounded downwards.

[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]4. How to remove the bonuses from a Shoulder Sash
When you add a wrong Weapon to your Sash or the Shoulder Sash hat the wrong bonuses there is only 1 option:
To remove the bonuses of your Shoulder Sash with the Certificate of Reversal which can be bought in the itemshop.
The Certificate of Reversal will remove all bonuses from the Shoulder Sash.
You will not get the weapon back which got absorbed from the Shoulder Sash.
