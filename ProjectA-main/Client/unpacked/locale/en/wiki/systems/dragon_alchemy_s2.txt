[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]1. General Informations

The Dragon Stone Alchemy is from level 1, you can equip it and activate it. You can get Dragon Stones 
from Cor Draconis. The Dragon Stone Alchemy Window can be opened via the green button in the inventory 
or with the button "O". The first Dragon Stone Alchemy site can be activated and deactivated with "STRG + O" 
and the second with "STRG + P". Dragon Stones can be obtained from Cor Draconis which drops from metinstones 
and bosses.

[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]3. Aspects of the Dragon Stones 

The Dragon Stones have 3 different aspects: class, clarity and level.
Every aspect of them is important and boosts the Dragon Stone.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]3.1 The Class
The Dragon Stone have 5 different classes, which can be upgraded:
> Class: Rough, Cut, Rare, Antique and Legendary.
The Dragon Stone gets more bonuses if the class gets higher.
To upgrade the class from a Dragon Stone you need a second Dragon Stone with the same class.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]3.1 The Clarity
The Dragon Stones have 5 different clarity's, which can be upgraded:
> Clarity: Matt, Clear, Flawless, Brilliant and Excellent.
The Dragon Stone gets higher bonuses if the clarity gets higher.
To upgrade the clarity from a Dragon Stone you need a second Dragon Stone with the same clarity.
It's possible to downgrade a excellent Dragon Stone to brilliant.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]3.2 The Level
A Dragon Stone on the clarity excellent can be upgraded up to +6.
The Level will be upgraded with Green Dragon Bean, Blue Dragon Bean and Pink Dragon Bean.

[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]4. Lifetime of the Dragon Stones

The Dragon Stones have a maximal lifetime of 24 hours which only counts when the Dragon Stone Alchemy is active.
You can fill the lifetime with Time Elixir.
The Dragon Stone will not disappear when he reaches 0 minutes.

[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=1535; CENTER_ALIGN]



[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]5. Dragon Stone Alchemy Set Bonus

When you activate 6 legendary Dragon Stones on the clarity "excellent" +6 the Dragon Stone Alchemy Set Bonus will activate.
The Set Bonus will give you following bonunses:

*Max. HP +1500
*Strong against Half Humans +5%
*Srong against Monster +5%
