[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]1. General Informations

A costume can have a maximum of 5 bonuses. Up to the 3rd bonus, they are added using the 
Reinforce Weapon Costume(), Reinforce Armor Costume() & Reinforce Hairstyle ().

The 4h and 5th bonuses are added using the 
Reinforce Weapon Costume+ (), Reinforce Armor Costume+ (), Reinforce Hairstyle+ ().

[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]5. Mount Costumes

Mount Costumes do not have bonuses but they offer a nice look on your mount. 
Also, some costumes offer a significant movement speed boost.

[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=1080; CENTER_ALIGN]


[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]6. How to transfer the bonuses of a Costume.

You can transfer the bonuses of a Costume to another using a 'Costume Bonus Transfer' at Theowahdan. 
The costume in the left slot will be emptied and the bonuses will be transfered to the costume in the right slot.

[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=1165; CENTER_ALIGN]


[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]7. How to remove the bonuses of a Costume.

You can remove the bonuses of a Costume using a 'Remove Costume Bonus'. This item will allow you to select 1 of the 
existing bonuses and remove it from the costume. You dont get the bonus back!
