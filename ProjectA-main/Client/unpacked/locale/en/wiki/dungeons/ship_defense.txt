[COLOR=0xffd700;FONT_SIZE=15]Peculiarity:
*The Ship Defense has 180 minutes cooldown.
*The Dungeon can be entered only with a group.
*You must complete the dungeon before the Mast is destroyed.
*You have to choose your reward from the Chest in the end of the dungeon.



[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18]The Phases:
[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=140; CENTER_ALIGN]
[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Phase 1

[X_PADDING=20]Kill all the monsters on the deck of the Ship and protect the Ship's Mast.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Phase 2

[X_PADDING=20]Kill all the monsters on the deck of the Ship and protect the Ship's Mast.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Phase 3

[X_PADDING=20]Kill all the monsters and metin stones on the deck. Once the Hydra Debuff is gone, 
[X_PADDING=20]you can defeat the first Hydra Head.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Phase 4

[X_PADDING=20]Kill all the monsters on the deck of the Ship and protect the Ship's Mast.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Phase 5

[X_PADDING=20]Kill all the monsters on the deck of the Ship and protect the Ship's Mast.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Phase 6

[X_PADDING=20]Kill all the monsters and metin stones on the deck. Once the Hydra Debuff is gone,
[X_PADDING=20]you can defeat the second Hydra Head.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Phase 7

[X_PADDING=20]Kill all the monsters on the deck of the Ship and protect the Ship's Mast.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Phase 8

[X_PADDING=20]Kill all the monsters on the deck of the Ship and protect the Ship's Mast.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Phase 9

[X_PADDING=20]Kill all the monsters and metin stones on the deck. Once the Hydra Debuff is gone, 
[X_PADDING=20]you can defeat the last Hydra Head. A Black Pearl Treasure Chest can be exchanged
[X_PADDING=20]for a Blue Crystal Essence from the Hydra Chest.
