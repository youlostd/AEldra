[COLOR=0xffd700;FONT_SIZE=15]Requirements:
*Required Level: 75
*Entrance Ticket: You don't need a group, but you have to belong to a guild

[COLOR=0xffd700;FONT_SIZE=15]Peculiarity:
*Meley's Hort has 180 minutes cooldown.
*You have to be in a Guild to enter the Dungeon and you need at least 4 Characters.
*You have to choose your reward from the Chest in the end of the dungeon. There are two chests to choose from.
*Up to 16 different characters can enter the dungeon.


[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18]The Phases:
[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=135; CENTER_ALIGN]
[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Phase 1

[X_PADDING=20]Damage the Dragon Statues until they have only 75% of their HP left. 
[X_PADDING=20]After that, an orange shield appears above the Statue and it cannot be further damaged.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Phase 2

[X_PADDING=20]Damage the Dragon Statues until they have only 50% of their HP. 
[X_PADDING=20]Four dragon Eggs appear every two minutes and as long as they are alive, the Statues receive no damage.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Phase 3

[X_PADDING=20]Four Dragon Watcher Idall appear and they must be defeated. The Dragon Statues can then be 
[X_PADDING=20]damaged up to 0%. Four Dragon Eggs appear every two Minutes and as long as they are alive, 
[X_PADDING=20]the Statues receive no damage. Upon reaching 0% HP, the statues will become green.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Phase 4

[X_PADDING=20]Once all four Dragon Statues have reached 0% HP, four players must drag 
[X_PADDING=20]a Dragon Fire Ring on the Statues within 10 Seconds. Meley is defeated and the Statues disappear. 
[X_PADDING=20]After that, the Golden Dragon Chest appears from which you can choose your Reward.
