[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]1. General Information.

You can see your actual Gaya balance left to your Yang balance.
Gaya can be traded with a Gaya Voucher from the Gaya Market.

[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=165; CENTER_ALIGN]


[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]2. How can I get Gaya?

Gaya can be crafted at the Gaya Merchant with 10 Gemstones, 500.000 Yang an 1 Stone +0 - 3.
There is the option to craft 10 Gaya at one time, for this you need 100 Gemstones, 5.000.000 Yang an 10 Stones +0 - 3.
To craft Gaya is always 100% and can't fail.

[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_small.tga; Y=260; CENTER_ALIGN]

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]2.1 How can I get Gemstones?

Gemstones can be obtained from most of the bosses and boss chests, also from events.

[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=330; CENTER_ALIGN]


[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]3. What can I buy with Gaya?

With Gaya you can buy some valuable items at the Gaya Market which always have 12 items.
