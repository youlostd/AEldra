[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]1. General Informations
[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=100; CENTER_ALIGN]

[CENTER_ALIGN]Costumes can be obtained from the Battlepass, Events oder Players.
[CENTER_ALIGN]We have 3 different costumes: Hairstyle, Armorcostume and Weaponcostumes. All these Costumes are Permanent.
[CENTER_ALIGN]A Costume can have 5 bonuses. The Costumes need different Costume Adder and Changer.



[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]2. Costume Bonus Adder & Changer
[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=195; CENTER_ALIGN]

[CENTER_ALIGN]To add the first 3 bonuses you need a Costume Reinforce Item without +.
[CENTER_ALIGN]To add the 4. and 5. bonus you need a Costume Reinforce Item with +.
[CENTER_ALIGN]The 4. and 5. bonus can only be added when the first 3 bonuses got added to the Costume.
[CENTER_ALIGN]It will always add only 1 bonus, also this is 100%.

[CENTER_ALIGN]To change the first 3 bonuses you need Costume Enchant Item without +.
[CENTER_ALIGN]To change the 4. and 5. bonus you need a Costume Enchant Item with +.

[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]6. Costume Bonus Transfer
[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=1300; CENTER_ALIGN]

[CENTER_ALIGN]Its possible to transfer the bonuses of a Costume with a Costume Bonus Transfer at Theowahdan to a other Costume.
[CENTER_ALIGN]The Costume in the left slot will be emptied and the bonuses will be transfered to the Costume in the right slot.
[CENTER_ALIGN]When you want to transfer the bonuses on a Costume which has bonuses already
[CENTER_ALIGN]the old bonuses will be overwritten with the new ones.
