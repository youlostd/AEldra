[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18]The Phases:
[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=132; CENTER_ALIGN]
[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Level 1

[X_PADDING=20]Summon 100 Monsters within 90 Seconds. Killing monsters will spawn more and more.
[X_PADDING=20]For each monster killed, twice the number appears.
[X_PADDING=20]Once 100 monsters have been summoned, you have 60 Seconds to kill them all.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Level 2

[X_PADDING=20]Destroy the right Metin Stone.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Level 3

[X_PADDING=20]Collect Keys of the Forest from the upcoming monster waves. 
[X_PADDING=20]The Keys of the Forest are needed to destroy Sinarin's Offspring.
[X_PADDING=20]Each Key deducts 10% of Offspring's life.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Level 4

[X_PADDING=20]Kill Sindarin.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Level 5

[X_PADDING=20]Survive 3 Minutes and defeat all the monsters. Every 30 Seconds a Saze Zone appears on the ground. 
[X_PADDING=20]If you don't reach it on time, the level restarts.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Level 6

[X_PADDING=20]Defeat all of the 3 monster waves within 3 minutes. If you fail, you will restart the level.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Level 7

[X_PADDING=20]Defeat Thranduil.
[X_PADDING=20]After Thranduil is defeated, there is a 10% chance that you will summon the Guardian of the Forest. 
[X_PADDING=20]You can craft the new PvP equipment from him.
