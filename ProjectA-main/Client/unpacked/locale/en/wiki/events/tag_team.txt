[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]1. General Information:

The Tag Team Event is a PvP Battle that is performed in different groups.
Each of these groups consists of 7 teams, 2 Players for each Team!
Both players of the winning Team will be rewarded at the end of a round.
You can sign up with your partner in Map1 at the Captain. Be sure that you are near to your Tag Team Partner.
There's also a Spectator Mode available for the Tag Team Event.


[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=220; CENTER_ALIGN]

[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]2. Event Instructions:

When you register for the Event with your partner, you will be teleported into a Special Map.
In this map, all the potions and Elixirs are disabled.
Running towards the middle part of the map, you will meet the other 6 teams, 12 players.
The team to survive with at least one (1) member alive will win the round and enjoy the rewards!


[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=330; CENTER_ALIGN]

[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]3. Event Rewards:

The Tag Team Rewards are announced usually in the "Events" section of the forum. 
They depend on the players' needs and change from Event to Event.
