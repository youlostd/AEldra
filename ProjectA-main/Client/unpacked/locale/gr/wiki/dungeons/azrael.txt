[COLOR=0xffd700;FONT_SIZE=15]Peculiarity:
*The Devil's Catacomb is both accessible with and without a group. 
*Within an hour, the Dungeon must be completed.
*With a Boss Room Ticket (Azrael) you get straight into the last level and you can kill Azrael.
*The Devil's Catacomb has a 60-minutes cooldown.


[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18]The Levels:
[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=145; CENTER_ALIGN]

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Level 1

[X_PADDING=20]Kill monsters until you drop a Soul Crystal Key. 
[X_PADDING=20]You can must drag the key to the Kud Statue in the end of this floor.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Level 2

[X_PADDING=20]You must fight your way through the Gate of Perdition until you reach the 
[X_PADDING=20]Tortoise Rock in the end of the floor. Only one person needs to click on the Tortoise. 
[X_PADDING=20](All group members need a Shrunken Head)

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Level 3

[X_PADDING=20]In this floor you'll find 7 metinstones, called Metin of Retaliation, 
[X_PADDING=20]which must be destroyed. There is only 1 correct that will allow you to move to the next level.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Level 4

[X_PADDING=20]Find and kill Tartaros, he is in one of the rooms beneath the temple. 
[X_PADDING=20]Take the dropped Grimace Totem to the top of the temple, drag & drop it to the Basalt Obelisk.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Level 5

[X_PADDING=20]Kill all the monsters on this floor, then, focus on Charon. After all the monsters have been defeated, 
[X_PADDING=20]you will move on to the next level.

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]Level 6

[X_PADDING=20]Kill Azrael
