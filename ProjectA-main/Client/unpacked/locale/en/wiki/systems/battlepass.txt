[CENTER_ALIGN] Battlepass is a series of missions which are rewarded upon successful completion.

[COLOR=0xffd700;FONT_SIZE=15]Content List:
*1. Monthly Battlepass
*2. Starterpass
*3. Battlepass of the Tree
*4. Battlepass (Dungeons)

[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]1. Monthly Battlepass

[CENTER_ALIGN]In the beginning of each month, a Battlepass is on sale from the Item Shop which, like the rest Battlepasses,
[CENTER_ALIGN]contains 7 normal missions and 3 Legendary missions. The highlight of each Battlepass is usually 
[CENTER_ALIGN]2x Costume Vouchers along with many other rewards. The Monthly Battlepass is NOT tradeable and it is limited
[CENTER_ALIGN]to 1 per ID. You can find informations about the newest battlepass at our board at the Itemshop News

[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]2. Starterpass

[CENTER_ALIGN]By clicking this item, you will receive 7 normal missions & 3 Legendary missions. 
[CENTER_ALIGN]This is probably the most important feature you have as a beginner. Completing 
[CENTER_ALIGN]each mission is quite simple and will reward you with fundamental equipment, such as: 
[CENTER_ALIGN]Rune Items, Master Sash(custom), Cor Draconis(Legendary), etc... Make sure to start 
[CENTER_ALIGN]the missions as soon as possible.


[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]2.1.1 The Worries of Uriel
[X_PADDING=20]Quest: Talk to Uriel
[X_PADDING=20]Reward: 100x Green Magic, 20x Green Strength

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]2.1.2 The Skills
[X_PADDING=20]Quest: Defeat 30 Map2 Metinstones
[X_PADDING=20]Reward: 10x Emerald Crystal, 20x Exorcism Scroll, 20x Concentrated Reading

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]2.1.3 Boss Monsters
[X_PADDING=20]Quest: Destroy 30 low level bosses (Orc Chief, Spider Queen, Flame King...)
[X_PADDING=20]Reward: 3x Experience Ring+, 1x Thief's Glove, 10x White Dew, 5x Soul Stone

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]2.1.4 The Mission Books
[X_PADDING=20]Quest: Find and complete 5 Mission Books. Can be any grade
[X_PADDING=20]Reward: 25x Dragon God Defense, 25x Dragon God Attack, 25x Dragon God Life, 
[X_PADDING=20]25x Dragon God Intelligence

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]2.1.5 The Red Forest
[X_PADDING=20]Quest: Destroy 100 Metinstones at Red Forest
[X_PADDING=20]Reward: Orc Parchment, Arachnids Key, Boss Room Ticket (Azrael)

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]2.1.6 Equipment Progress
[X_PADDING=20]Quest: Defeat 10 Boss Monster (Death Reaper, Azrael, Beran Setaou, Spider Baroness, Orc Maze Chief)
[X_PADDING=20]Reward: 200x Enchant Item, 25x Gemstone, 2x Potion of Thief, 2x Potion of Wisdom

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]2.1.7 The Runes
[X_PADDING=20]Quest: Destroy 50 Metinstone on the Lv90 Maps
[X_PADDING=20]Reward: 60x all rune items

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]2.1.8 Progress 1
[X_PADDING=20]Quest: Reach Lv50
[X_PADDING=20]Reward: 5x Cor Draconis (Legendary)

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]2.1.9 Progress 2
[X_PADDING=20]Quest: Reach Lv75
[X_PADDING=20]Reward: Master Sash(custom)[11%], 25x Gemstone

[COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]2.1.10 Progress 3
[X_PADDING=20]Quest: Reach Lv90
[X_PADDING=20]Reward: 10x Blacksmith Handbook, 10x Potion of Oblivion, 10x Researcher's Elixir
